# Questions worth answering (for you two)

Use this list to capture details over time. Answers can be folded into `functions/knowledge/chunks.json` for LoveGPT, or kept private between you.

## Daily life & rhythm

- **What book are you reading together right now?** (title, author if it helps — update the knowledge base when you finish and start a new one.)
- **What football or sports team do you support** — and do you watch matches or highlights together?
- What does a “normal” weekday look like for each of you (wake time, work/school, wind-down)?
- What are your non-negotiables for feeling connected when long distance (calls, texts, voice notes)?
- What does “quality time” mean to each of you — same thing or different?

## Money & home (future)

- How do you imagine splitting finances after marriage — fully shared, partly shared, or step-by-step?
- What’s one money habit you admire in the other, and one you’d like to grow in together?
- City vs. suburbs vs. country: what does “home base” mean for you in five years?

## Conflict & repair

- What’s your go-to when you feel hurt — withdraw, explain, fix fast, or something else?
- What’s a phrase or gesture that reliably helps you feel safe again after a disagreement?
- What topics feel hardest to talk about, and how do you want to approach them?

## Faith & values

- How do you want prayer and church community to show up in a shared household?
- What traditions from each family do you want to keep, blend, or gently leave behind?

## Family & boundaries

- How often do you ideally see parents/siblings, and how do you protect your own couple space?
- What does healthy extended-family involvement look like for you?

## Fun & adventure

- Three “small adventures” you want this year (not necessarily expensive).
- A trip you both want to take before kids (or before the next big life step).
- Indoor vs. outdoor: what recharges each of you?

## Wedding & celebration (when the time comes)

- Big wedding vs. intimate — what matters most: people, food, music, vows, photos?
- Any cultural or church elements that are must-haves for either of you?

## Inside jokes & language

- A few phrases, nicknames, or references only you two understand (safe to store if you want LoveGPT to “get” you).
- Songs, movies, or moments that instantly mean “us.”

## Growth edges (honest)

- One thing you’re proud of in how you love each other, and one thing you’re working on.
- What “being known” looks like for you — what you’re still learning to share.
